Rate Plans

Base

The Base plan offers GPS location, VIN, Driver ID, and basic IOX support.

HOS

The Hos plan adds Hours of Service functionality over the Base plan. It is geared to fleets that are primarily concerned about HOS compliance.

Pro

The Pro plan offers the functionality of the HOS plan and adds support for engine and accelerometer data.

Pro Plus

The Pro plan offers the greatest functionality, including Active Tracking, a lifetimewarranty, and premium services.
